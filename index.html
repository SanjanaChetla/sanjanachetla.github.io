I’m Sanjana chetla


A detail-oriented individual with a Bachelor's in Design from NIFT skilled in textile design, product design, design thinking, and user research. Traweller

UX project & Visuals

The fundamental concept of this project is for the users who find difficulty in knowing the routes and timings of public transportation in Hyderabad. This smartphone application is designed for both newcomers and long-term city residents. This application will not only help users identify the routes and stations of all local buses, metros, and trains, but it will also assist them in purchasing tickets, Finding the best route and identifying timings.

Diagnostic Study of GI registered Handicraft  Srikalahasti Kalamkari

Research Assistant

As a Research Assistant, I assisted Prof. Jyothirmai Singothu in doing diagnostic research on the GI-registered handicraft Srikalahasti Kalamkari. I studied the craft, conducted many interviews with artisans regarding their understanding of geographical indication, and documented the findings. The findings of the study have been submitted to the Ministry of Textiles.
