I’m Sanjana Chetla

A detail-oriented individual with a Bachelor's in Design from NIFT skilled in textile design, product design, design thinking, and user research. Passionate about designing experiences that positively impact people, the environment, and society. I prefer working in a collaborative ecosystem, as the design isn't a one-man show; it needs an understanding of the user and their environment. I believe in " trust the process," as designing is all about the process that goes into creating the final product.

I am currently learning and expanding my knowledge in the field of user experience design.
